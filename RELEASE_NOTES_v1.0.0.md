We're excited to announce the first official release of **TabSyncerAI** - the multi-panel AI workspace that lets you broadcast prompts to multiple AI assistants simultaneously!

## üéØ What's New

### Core Features
- **Multi-Panel Interface** - Work with ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, DeepSeek, Grok, Copilot, Perplexity, and Mistral side-by-side
- **Prompt Broadcasting** - Type once, send to all selected AI services
- **File Upload Support** - Attach images and documents to your prompts
- **Persistent Sessions** - Stay logged in across app restarts
- **Drag & Drop** - Reorder panels to match your workflow

### User Experience
- **Chat-Style Interface** - Familiar messenger-like control panel
- **Customizable Layout** - Adjust panel widths and configurations
- **Individual Controls** - Enable/disable, reload, or reset each panel independently
- **State Persistence** - Your window size and preferences are remembered
